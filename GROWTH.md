# GEO Tracker — Launch & Growth Kit

Grounded plan to get the first paying customers. No spam, no fake growth — those burn the brand.
The product itself is the best sales tool: **a free scan that shows a prospect they're invisible in AI answers.**

---

## The hook (use this everywhere)
> "When someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Gemini to recommend a *[your category]*, does your brand come up? Most brands have no idea. GEO Tracker shows you — and tracks it over time."

## Who actually pays (rank order)
1. **SEO / marketing agencies** — they resell visibility to clients; Agency tier ($149) is built for them. *Highest LTV, fastest close.*
2. **DTC / SaaS brands** doing content marketing who already care about search.
3. **Founders / solo marketers** watching competitors.

## Lead magnet = the product
The free tier already runs a real scan. Make that the entire top of funnel: "Free AI Visibility Report for your brand." Every channel below drives to a free scan, not a signup wall.

## Week-1 plan (in order of speed-to-cash)

### 1. Direct outreach (fastest revenue — do this daily)
Pick 20 agencies/brands a day. Run a free scan of THEIR brand first, then send a personalized note with the actual result. This isn't spam — it's a relevant, specific insight they'll want. Template below. Respect opt-outs, don't bulk-blast.

### 2. Product Hunt launch (traffic spike)
Assets ready below. Launch Tue–Thu 12:01am PT. Line up 10 people to comment early.

### 3. Communities (value-first, not "check out my product")
r/SEO, r/marketing, r/juststart, Indie Hackers, X SEO circles. Post a genuine *finding* ("I scanned 50 brands to see who AI recommends — here's the pattern"), product mentioned once at the end.

### 4. SEO content (compounds over weeks)
One cornerstone post: "How to check if your brand appears in ChatGPT / Perplexity / Gemini answers." Ranks for the exact intent, links to a free scan.
